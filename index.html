Our Special Pastries

Each pastry assures an unforgettable tasting journey, fusing innovation with tradition to enchant your palate.

Cheesy Ensaymada

Fluffy ensaymada dough infused with rich buttery goodness and hints of vanilla, topped with a generous sprinkle of grated Edam cheese for a delightful twist on a classic Filipino pastry.

Cinnamon Roll

Soft and pillowy cinnamon roll dough, swirled with a luscious mixture of cinnamon, brown sugar, and butter, creating layers of sweet, aromatic goodness. Topped with a creamy vanilla glaze for an irresistible treat that's perfect for breakfast or dessert.

Cheesebread

Fluffy cheesebread dough, enriched with a savory blend of grated Cheddar cheese, butter, and a hint of garlic, offering a delightful burst of cheesy goodness in every bite. Enjoyed warm and fresh from the oven, it's a savory delight that satisfies any craving.
